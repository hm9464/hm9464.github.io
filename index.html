About Me

Background

Born and raised in Hong Kong, I moved to the US to study Finance at Boston University. Upon graduation, I moved to Washington DC to pursue a masters degree and begin my pursuit to work in technology.

Why Data Science?

Like many in this field, I didn't intend on finding a career specifically in data science. I took a class during my Masters degree in analytics which prompted me to learn Python and R, as well as the basics of statistics, machine learning, and algorithms.

What I Do Today?

I now work as a consultant, helping clients build out data pipelines, design architectures for data engineering projects, and work on creating predictive analytics platforms to help gain insight into my clients' businesses. I have worked on a wide variety of projects, including machine learning, cloud infrastructure architecture, DevSecOps, and web development.
